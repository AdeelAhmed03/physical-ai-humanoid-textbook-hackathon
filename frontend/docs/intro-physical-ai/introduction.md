# Introduction to Physical AI

Physical AI represents a convergence of artificial intelligence and physical systems. This field explores how AI algorithms can be integrated with physical robots and environments to create intelligent agents that can perceive, reason, and act in the real world.

## Key Concepts

Physical AI combines several key areas of research:

- Perception systems that can understand the physical world
- Reasoning systems that can model physical interactions
- Action systems that can manipulate physical objects
- Learning systems that can adapt to physical environments

## Applications

Physical AI has applications in numerous fields including:
- Autonomous vehicles
- Industrial robotics
- Healthcare robotics
- Domestic robotics
- Agricultural robotics

## Challenges

Developing Physical AI systems presents unique challenges:
- Real-time processing requirements
- Safety and reliability concerns
- Physical world uncertainty
- Complex interaction dynamics
